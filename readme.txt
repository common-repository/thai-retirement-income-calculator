Our retirement income calculator allows visitors to easily calculate if their income is sufficient for a Thai retirement visa.

== Description ==

‘How do I work out if my income is enough to qualify for a Thai Retirement Visa?’ - That question is asked many times every year, and equally there are many new websites and blogs appearing which provide information about Thai retirement visas.

So, in order to answer that question easily we have developed a plugin called Thai Retirement Income Calculator.

= What does our plugin do? =

Our retirement income calculator can be added to any wordpress website or blog allowing visitors to easily calculate if their income is sufficient for a Thai retirement visa or whether they need to supplement their income with funds deposited in a Thai bank account.

The calculator is simply a wordpress plugin which can be downloaded here and installed directly onto your site.

== Installation ==


1. Unzip retirethailand.zip and upload the folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Visit the "Retire Thailand" page under the settings menu to choose your options

Visit our website for a installation video guide {INSERT URL http://retirethailand.info/}


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who should install our plugin =

Anyone who offers services for retirees in Thailand including retirement visa processing, blogs on retiring in Thailand and even Real Estate agents who promote property sales and rentals to expat retirees.

= What are the current retirement visa regulations in Thailand? =

* You need to be over 50 years old
* Have monthly income from pensions/investments or other sources equating to 65,000 THB per month
* -OR- You must deposit a minimum of 800,000 THB in a Thai bank account – the money must be seasoned for two months on your first retirement extension and three months for subsequent extensions
* If your monthly income is not sufficient, you may combine the methods and ‘top up’ your monthly income with money deposited in the bank.
* Use our calculator to find out if your monthly income is sufficient to meet the requirements for a retirement visa in Thailand.
